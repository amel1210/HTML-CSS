About Us

Korea is famous for the food they will serve. They believe food should satisfy all senses, not just fill the stomach. They apply the balance between yin and yang in producing healthy and proportionate food, thus forming a beautiful and enjoyable art.

That's why we chose the name Munjialji, which means Rainbow, because we wanted our restaurant to have a philosophy like a rainbow, a work of art from God.
