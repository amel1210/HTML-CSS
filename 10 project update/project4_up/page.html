ARMY Bomb in Details

The size of the third version of the ARMY Bomb is 98 x 98 x 224 so it's smaller than the both versions 1 and 2. It is made of ABS, PC and silicon and weights approximately 150g. In the package you will find - beside the light stick - a strap, nonwoven bag, user manual and a quality guarantee card.
